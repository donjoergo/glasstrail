GlassTrail is a drink-tracking app for logging what you drink, reviewing your habits in statistics, and staying connected with friends through a shared feed.

Log a drink in seconds with a photo, location, and comment, then look back at your history in a personal feed. Statistics break your habits down by streaks, category, map, and gallery, so you can see patterns over time instead of just raw numbers.

Add friends with shareable profile links, follow their drink entries, comments, and photos in a social feed, and get notified about friend requests, friendship updates, and new drinks from people you follow.

Features:

* Drink logging with photo, location, and comment
* Personal feed of your own drink history
* Friends with shareable profile links and private connections
* Social feed with friends' entries, comments, photos, and locations
* In-app and push notifications for friend and drink activity
* Statistics with streaks, category breakdown, map, and gallery
* Global drink catalog plus your own custom drinks
* Bar management with sorting, visibility controls, and custom drinks
* Left-handed mode
* Multi-device sync for drinks, statistics, and profile
* English and German localization
